About the Collection

A Wintry Piano Wonderland transforms beloved Christmas carols into sophisticated piano arrangements that capture both the nostalgia and wonder of the holiday season.

Each piece is carefully crafted to honor the original melodies while introducing fresh harmonic textures and expressive nuances that make these timeless songs feel new again.

Accompanied by charming illustrations, this collection creates a complete sensory experience that brings families together around the piano during the most magical time of year.

About Oded Essner

Oded Essner is a passionate pianist and arranger whose love for classical music meets the warmth of holiday traditions.

With years of experience in creating accessible yet sophisticated piano arrangements, Oded brings a unique perspective that honors musical heritage while making it approachable for pianists of all levels.

His arrangements have been celebrated for their ability to capture the emotional essence of beloved melodies while providing pianists with rewarding musical challenges.
